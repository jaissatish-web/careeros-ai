# Gulf Career Knowledge Base

## Visa Categories

### Saudi Arabia (Nitaqat System)
- **Red Category**: Expats in excess - quotas reduced
- **Yellow Category**: Balanced workforce - stable hiring
- **Green Category**: Saudi majority - priority hiring
- **Platinum Category**: Saudization exceeded - open hiring

### UAE (Golden Visa)
- 10-year residence visa for investors, professionals, researchers
- Requires minimum $270K investment or specialized skills
- Family included, no sponsor required

### Qatar (Qatarization)
- Nationalization program for Qatari citizens
- Priority for government sector positions
- 5-year renewable work visas for expats

### Kuwait (Demographic Imbalance)
- Strict expat quotas in public sector
- Private sector more open
- Kuwaitization program under implementation

## Labor Law Key Points

### Working Hours
- GCC Standard: 8 hours/day, 48 hours/week
- Ramadan: Reduced hours (6-7 hours/day)
- Friday mandatory rest in most Gulf countries

### Annual Leave
- **Saudi Arabia**: 21 days minimum, increases with tenure
- **UAE**: 30 days minimum
- **Qatar**: 21 days minimum
- **Kuwait**: 30 days minimum
- **Bahrain**: 21 days minimum
- **Oman**: 30 days minimum

### End of Service Benefits
- 21+ days salary per year for first 5 years
- 30 days per year after 5 years
- Gratuity payment upon contract completion
